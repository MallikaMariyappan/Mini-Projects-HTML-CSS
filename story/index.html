The Town Mouse And The Country House

Once, a town mouse visited its cousin, the country mouse. The country mouse was happy to see him. The town mouse was not happy with the simple lifestyle of his cousin. "Come home with me," he said. The country mouse was excited. He had never been to a town. The country mouse was amazed to see the tall buildings, The town mouse took him to a house. The table had been laid with wonderful dishes.

Just as they had started eating, a woman came with a broom. Scared, the country mouse ran all the way back home. He was better offin the country than in the town.
